Empowering Students to Succeed

Bison Prof is the premier peer-to-peer tutoring platform designed exclusively for University of Manitoba students.

Our Mission

At Bison Prof, we believe that the best way to learn is from those who have walked the path before you. Our mission is to connect students needing academic support with talented peers who have mastered the material.

We aim to foster a collaborative community where knowledge is shared, academic hurdles are overcome, and every student has the resources they need to achieve excellence.
