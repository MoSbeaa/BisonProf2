How can we help?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a tutor?
You can find a tutor by browsing the 'Tutors' page, filtering by major or course, or using the search bar on the homepage. Once you find a profile you like, simply click 'Book Session'.
How do payments work?
Payments are processed securely through our platform. You will be charged once the tutor confirms the session. We accept major credit cards and PayPal.
Can I become a tutor?
Absolutely! If you have a strong academic record, you can apply to become a tutor by clicking "Become a Tutor" in the navigation bar and filling out the application form.
What if I need to cancel a session?
You can cancel or reschedule a session from your dashboard up to 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee.
