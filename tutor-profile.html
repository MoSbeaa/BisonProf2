About Me

Hi! I'm Sarah, a 4th-year Computer Science Honours Student at the University of Manitoba with a 4.2 GPA. I specialize in helping 1st and 2nd-year students master the fundamentals of programming, algorithms, and data structures.

I have completed three Co-op work terms as a Software Developer, so I bring real-world industry knowledge to my tutoring sessions. I know exactly what professors are looking for in assignments and how to ace the exams for courses like COMP 1010, COMP 1020, and COMP 2140.

My goal is to make complex coding concepts easy to understand. Whether you are struggling with recursion in Java, pointers in C, or mathematical proofs in Discrete Math, I can help you build the logic and confidence you need to succeed.

About the lesson

Code Review Assignment Help Exam Prep Debugging

My sessions are practical and interactive. We won't just read slides; we will write code, debug errors, and solve problems together. I adapt my teaching style to how you learn best.

How I Can Help You:

  • Conceptual Clarity: Breaking down big topics like Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) and Data Structures.
  • Assignment Strategy: Learning how to approach complex coding assignments and write clean, efficient code.
  • Exam Preparation: Solving past midterm and final exams to practice speed and accuracy.
  • Languages: Java, Python, C, C++, JavaScript, HTML/CSS.
